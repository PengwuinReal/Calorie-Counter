Welcome to ForkCast!

Track your meals with just a snap! Our app uses AI to identify food from photos and estimate calories and nutrition instantly.

Features

  • Food Classification: Our model identifies 101 different types of foods using AI image identification.
  • Nutrition Analysis: Calorie, fat, carbs, and protein info pulled from Spoonacular API.
  • Easy Interface: Camera, stats, and homepage are one click away with smooth navigation.
  • Track Your Calories: Your consumed calories are stored in the Stats page for you to track.

Get Started

Go to the Camera tab to snap a picture and classify your food. Afterwards, glance at your daily food stats in the Stats page.

Our Mission

At ForkCast, our mission is to make healthy living more accessible through technology. We believe that nutrition tracking should be effortless and accurate. By combining AI food identifiers and trusted nutritional data, we aim to empower users to make better food choices — anytime, anywhere.
